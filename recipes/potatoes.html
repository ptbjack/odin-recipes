Oven-baked Spicy Potatoes

Description

This recipe is a household staple, really easy to make and super delicious. Don't go easy on the garlic!

Ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tea spoon oregano
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220C.
  2. Chop garlic and put on low heat with a bunch of olive oil. Remove before it turns brown. Let it cool for 5 minutes.
  3. Slice potatoes into thin-ish wedges. Add them to a bowl.
  4. Add garlic and spices to the potatoes.
  5. Mix until slices are equally covered in oil and spices.
  6. Pour on a baking tray, line slices up so that they are not on top of each other.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, turning the slices over once in the middle of the process.
